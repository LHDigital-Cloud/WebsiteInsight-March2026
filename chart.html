Gap Analysis
Where Are They Lagging? — Detailed Breakdown
A clear analysis of where Hyundai at the national level and Lotus Hyundai at the dealer level are falling behind, and what actions can close each gap.
🏢
National Level
Hyundai India — Where It Lags
Hyundai.com/in tracks only a single URL and records just 220K monthly visits — while Tata Motors reaches 3.3M with a full multi-page strategy. Here is every gap, clearly explained.
Traffic Volume — 15× below Tata. Hyundai has 220K visits vs Tata's 3.3M. Both are top car brands in India. The gap is entirely due to SEO strategy, not brand popularity.
Single URL Tracking. Hyundai.com/in only measures one URL. Tata, Maruti, Toyota, Honda all measure the entire domain including subdomains — giving them 10× more indexable content.
No Model-Level Pages in Top Traffic. Honda's Elevate page alone gets 119K visits. Toyota's Hyryder page gets 361K. Hyundai has no model-specific page appearing in top traffic — a massive keyword opportunity missed.
Traffic Value Far Below Market Share. Hyundai holds 15–17% of India's car market, but its $4.7K traffic value is 18× below Maruti's $84.9K. This means Hyundai is not capturing the online research traffic that leads to showroom visits.
Flat Traffic Trend — No Growth. From Nov to March, Hyundai's line is flat at 220K while Tata grows from 3M to 3.6M. Hyundai is not investing in SEO content at scale.
No Price/EMI Content Visible. Maruti has an Ertiga price page ranking high. Toyota has a Fortuner page. These "on-road price" searches have very high purchase intent. Hyundai.com has none in top results.
Opportunity: Brand keyword volume is 223K/month. The search "hyundai" and "hyundai india" alone bring 244K monthly searches. Hyundai ranks #1 for these but fails to convert them into deeper page visits.
🏠
Dealer Level — Coimbatore
Lotus Hyundai — Where It Lags
lotushyundai.in is growing well with 444 visits/month and a sharp upward curve — but several gaps remain that are preventing 2× to 5× traffic growth in the next 90 days.
Strong Foundation — Growing Fast. 444 visits/month with near-tripling from Nov to March. Creta + Venue on-road price pages are working. This is the right content strategy — needs to be expanded immediately.
"Hyundai Showroom Coimbatore" — Not Ranking. This is the single most valuable local keyword (150 monthly searches, purchase intent). Kovai Hyundai ranks #6. Chandra ranks #8. Lotus does not appear in top 10 at all.
Hyperlocal Domain Near Zero. lotus-hyundai-coimbatore.com has just 1 visit/month. This domain ranks #7 for "showroom in coimbatore" — but without content, that rank will be lost. Needs 5+ pages immediately.
7 High-Traffic Model Pages Still Missing. Alcazar, Exter, Tucson, i20 N-Line, Verna — none have dedicated on-road price pages for Coimbatore. Each page = ~50–150 new monthly visits. Total opportunity: 500+ additional visits/month.
No Service Centre Page. "Hyundai service center coimbatore" gets 150 monthly searches. Chandra ranks #3 for this. Lotus has no service centre landing page — leaving this high-intent traffic entirely to a competitor.
Venue Brochure PDF Listed in Top Pages (14%). A PDF upload is ranking and sending traffic — not a proper webpage. This traffic is hard to convert and will fall once Google de-indexes the PDF. Replace with a proper Venue page.
No EMI Calculator — 2,400 Monthly Searches Missed. "Hyundai EMI calculator" gets 2.4K monthly searches. No Hyundai dealer in Coimbatore offers this tool. First to publish it captures all 2,400 searches.
